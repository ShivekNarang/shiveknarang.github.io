Projects

Evolutionary Genomics Research: Primate Phylogenetics
I conducted a detailed analysis of the evolutionary relationships among baboon species (Papio genus) by leveraging advanced bioinformatics tools and computational techniques. Using mitochondrial DNA (mtDNA) data, I constructed phylogenetic trees, calculated pairwise genetic distances, and assessed evolutionary rates to uncover maternal lineage relationships and divergence patterns. This research utilized technologies such as NCBI GenBank for sequence retrieval, MAFFT for multiple sequence alignment, and MEGA11 for phylogenetic tree construction and evolutionary distance calculations. Python, combined with the pandas library, was employed to extract and analyze branch lengths from phylogenetic tree data.

GlucaLink: Digital Health Solution for Prenatal Diabetes Management
I co-developed GlucaLink, a digital health platform designed to optimize diabetes management during pregnancy by consolidating remote glucose monitoring, reducing provider burden, and improving maternal and neonatal outcomes. The system integrates real-time alerts, predictive analytics, patient-specific reminders, and an accessible, user-friendly interface. We used Python for automation, Figma for interface design, and machine learning for predictive care. GlucaLink addresses gaps in current care by reducing manual errors, enhancing user experience, and supporting scalability for broader adoption in prenatal care.

Stage Synthesis of 1-BROMO-3-CHLORO-5-IODOBENZENE from Aniline
This paper outlines a six-stage synthesis reaction for the conversion of aniline into 1-bromo-3-chloro-5-iodobenzene. The process involves acetylation to protect the amine group, followed by bromination and chlorination in successive steps. Hydrolysis is then employed to remove the protecting group, and iodination is carried out, concluding with deamination to eliminate the amine. Electrophilic aromatic substitution (EAS) with ortho, para, and meta directing groups directs the addition of various halogen groups. The products are extracted through recrystallization and vacuum filtration. Purity is ensured through melting point, 1HNMR, and IR spectra analyses at each stage, resulting in an overall synthesis yield of 8.09%.

The Digital Transformation of Health Care
This paper analyzes the digital health strategies of four major tech companies—Google, Microsoft, Amazon, and Apple—based on their recent market activities (acquisitions, new products/services, partnerships, and key hires). Our project aims to: 1. Define each company's digital health strategy. 2. Outline each company's strategic strengths and risk factors. 3. Identify their potential and actual competitors. Our objective is to determine which of these companies is best positioned for success in the digital health field.

Big Data Analytics-Machine Learning Model to classify Credit card Fraud
