Yelp Reviews in Healthcare: Sentiment Analysis and Predictive Modeling of Quality Ratings
This research explores the use of Yelp reviews to analyze a large dataset on healthcare institutions, focusing on sentiment analysis and predictive modeling of star ratings. It emphasizes the importance of patient-centric care and the challenges in quantifying healthcare quality. The paper discusses the correlation between Yelp reviews and healthcare quality, addressing the need for reliable and patient-focused tools in healthcare evaluation.

Stage Synthesis of 1-BROMO-3-CHLORO-5-IODOBENZENE from Aniline
This paper outlines a six-stage synthesis reaction for the conversion of aniline into 1-bromo-3-chloro-5-iodobenzene. The process involves acetylation to protect the amine group, followed by bromination and chlorination in successive steps. Hydrolysis is then employed to remove the protecting group, and iodination is carried out, concluding with deamination to eliminate the amine. Electrophilic aromatic substitution (EAS) with ortho, para, and meta directing groups directs the addition of various halogen groups. The products are extracted through recrystallization and vacuum filtration. Purity is ensured through melting point, 1HNMR, and IR spectra analyses at each stage, resulting in an overall synthesis yield of 8.09%.
